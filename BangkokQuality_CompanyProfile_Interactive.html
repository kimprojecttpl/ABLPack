What We Craft

Our core artisanal dairy line is crafted for both retail and HORECA food-service applications — using only the freshest natural ingredients.

🥛

Greek Yogurt

Authentic thick & creamy, 100% whole milk and live cultures, traditionally strained for sweet and savory dishes.

🍶

Plain Yogurt

Unsweetened, smooth and refreshing — versatile for drinking, cooking, or blending into beverages and sauces.

🥥

Coconut Yogurt (Vegan)

Creamy coconut yogurt with fresh coconut milk; perfect for breakfast bowls, smoothies and desserts.

🥄

Sour Cream

Rich, creamy traditional sour cream with a smooth texture — ideal for cooking, baking and sauces.

🥒

Tzatziki

Traditional Mediterranean dip of yogurt, cucumber and dill — for grilled dishes, wraps and breads.

🧀

Feta Cheese

Authentic Mediterranean feta, firm texture (in brine or olive oil), 500g pack — cubed or whole chunk.

🫓

Spreadable Labane

Creamy spreadable labane topped with aromatic za'atar — a dip or spread for breads and platters.

⚪

Labane Cheese Balls

Handcrafted labane balls with za'atar; firm, rich and creamy — ideal for Mediterranean platters.

Yogurt Culture — our retail brand — extends the line into Greek yogurt bowls, açaí, soft serve, smoothies and frozen-yogurt shakes (Ready to Glow, Energy Sip, Biscoff Shake, Oreo Choco Shake & more), doubling as a living showroom for the categories we source.
